SimplePomodoro.

A simple, pomodoro timer.

SimplePomodoro Says: The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method that involves working in focused 25-minute sessions (called “pomodoros”), followed by a short 5-minute break. After completing four pomodoros, a longer break of 15 to 30 minutes is taken. The goal is to improve concentration and reduce mental fatigue by breaking work into manageable intervals with regular rest.
